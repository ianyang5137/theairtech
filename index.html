About Us

关于我们

We are a professional air conditioning and ventilation service provider based in Auckland, New Zealand. We specialize in installation, repair, and maintenance of residential and commercial HVAC systems.

我们是新西兰奥克兰的专业空调与通风系统服务商，提供住宅和商用的安装、维修与维护服务。

We also offer expert repair services for a wide range of home appliances, including refrigerators, air conditioners, freezers, washing machines, televisions, ovens, induction cookers, and gas hobs.

我们还提供多种家电的维修服务，包括冰箱、空调、冷柜、洗衣机、电视、烤箱、电磁炉和打火灶。
