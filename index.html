Frequently Asked Questions

常见问题

What areas do you service? 你们服务哪些区域？

We provide services throughout the greater Auckland area, including North Shore, West Auckland, and South Auckland.

我们为大奥克兰地区提供服务，包括北岸、西区和南区。

How long does a typical repair take? 一般维修需要多长时间？

Most repairs can be completed within 1-2 hours. However, complex issues may require additional time or parts.

大多数维修可以在1-2小时内完成。但是，复杂的问题可能需要更多时间或零件。

Do you offer warranty on repairs? 维修有保修吗？

Yes, we offer a 30-day warranty on all our repairs and parts.

是的，我们为所有维修和零件提供30天保修。

We accept cash, credit cards, and bank transfers. Payment is due upon completion of the service.

我们接受现金、信用卡和银行转账。服务完成后付款。

Do you provide emergency services? 你们提供紧急服务吗？

Yes, we offer emergency repair services for urgent situations. Please call us directly for immediate assistance.
